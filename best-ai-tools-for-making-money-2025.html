The Best AI Tools for Making Money in 2025

1. Jasper AI — Best for Freelance Writers and Content Marketers

If you're serious about making money through writing, Jasper AI is arguably the most powerful tool in your arsenal. It's a long-form AI writing assistant trained specifically for marketing copy, blog content, email campaigns, and more.

How to make money with Jasper AI:

  • Freelance content writing: Offer blog posts, product descriptions, and SEO articles on platforms like Fiverr, Upwork, or PeoplePerHour. Jasper helps you deliver faster without sacrificing quality.
  • Content agency: Some UK freelancers have scaled into small content agencies by using Jasper to handle volume while they focus on editing and client relationships.

That means if you recommend it to others (say, through a blog or YouTube channel), you earn a cut every month they stay subscribed. Build a small audience and this becomes genuine passive income.

A Quick Note on Tax

If you're earning money using AI tools in the UK — whether from freelancing, affiliate commissions, or selling digital products — you'll need to register as self-employed and declare your income to HMRC. The threshold for self-assessment registration is £1,000 of trading income per tax year (the trading allowance). It's simple enough to manage, but don't ignore it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I really make money using AI tools in the UK?

Absolutely — thousands of UK freelancers, content creators, and small business owners already are. The key is treating AI as a productivity multiplier, not a magic money machine. You still need to provide value; AI just helps you do it faster and at greater scale.

Which AI tool is best for beginners?

Copy.ai is a great starting point thanks to its free tier and intuitive interface. If you're focused on longer content or building a writing-based income, Jasper AI is worth the investment fairly early on.

Do I need to tell HMRC about income from AI tools?

Yes — any income you earn (from freelancing, affiliate commissions, digital product sales, etc.) must be declared to HMRC if it exceeds the £1,000 trading allowance in a tax year. Keep records of your earnings and expenses from the start.

Is the affiliate income from tools like Jasper and Copy.ai worth pursuing?

If you already have a blog, newsletter, or social media following — even a modest one — absolutely. Copy.ai's 45% recurring commission and Jasper's 25% recurring commission mean that even a handful of active referrals can generate a meaningful monthly income stream with no additional work once the content is live.

How much can I realistically earn?

This varies enormously. A part-time freelance writer using AI tools might earn an extra £500–£1,500/month. A dedicated content creator or agency owner could earn significantly more. Affiliate income tends to start slowly but compounds nicely over time as your content ranks and your audience grows.
