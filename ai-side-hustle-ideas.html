Best AI Side Hustle Ideas to Earn Extra Money in 2024

Let's be honest — the cost of living isn't getting any easier, and most of us could do with a bit of extra cash coming in each month. Whether you're saving for a holiday, paying down debt, or just trying to keep up with the energy bills, a side hustle can make a real difference.

But here's the exciting bit: artificial intelligence has completely changed the game. You no longer need specialist skills, years of experience, or even much start-up capital to build a genuine income stream on the side. AI tools now do the heavy lifting — and all you need is a laptop, a few hours a week, and a willingness to learn.

In this post, we'll walk through the best AI side hustle ideas available to UK residents right now, what you can realistically earn, and exactly how to get started.

Why AI Side Hustles Are Worth Your Attention Right Now

AI isn't just a buzzword — it's a genuine productivity multiplier. Tasks that used to take hours now take minutes. Content that would have required expensive freelancers can now be drafted, edited, and published by a single person armed with the right tools.

For UK side hustlers, this creates a huge opportunity. You can offer professional-level services to clients without a professional-level background, or build digital income streams that run almost passively once they're set up.

And importantly, most of these ideas work whether you're in London, Leeds, or the Scottish Highlands — all you need is decent broadband.

The Best AI Side Hustle Ideas for UK Earners

1. AI-Assisted Freelance Copywriting

Freelance copywriting has always been a solid side hustle, but AI tools have dramatically lowered the barrier to entry — and boosted output for established writers.

Using a tool like Jasper AI, you can produce high-quality blog posts, product descriptions, email sequences, and social media content far faster than writing from scratch. Jasper is particularly strong for long-form content and has templates built specifically for marketing copy.

Realistic earning potential: £300–£1,500+ per month, depending on how many clients you take on and the niches you target (finance, health, and tech tend to pay best).

How to get started: - Sign up for Jasper AI and explore its templates - Build a small portfolio (you can create spec pieces for fictional brands) - List your services on platforms like PeoplePerHour or Bark.com - Pitch directly to small UK businesses that have poor website copy

💡 Affiliate tip: Jasper AI offers a 25% recurring commission on referrals — so if you end up recommending it to your own clients, you earn every month they stay subscribed.

2. Content Creation for Small Businesses

Millions of UK small businesses know they need regular content — blog posts, newsletters, social captions — but simply don't have the time or budget to hire a full marketing team. That's where you come in.

With tools like Copy.ai, you can handle the content needs of multiple small business clients simultaneously. Copy.ai is brilliant for shorter-form content: social posts, ad copy, product listings, and email subject lines. Its interface is beginner-friendly, and it has a generous free tier to get you started.

Realistic earning potential: £200–£800 per month per client retainer, with two or three clients putting you firmly in meaningful side income territory.

How to structure your offer: - A monthly retainer covering X blog posts + X social posts - One-off content audits and rewrites - Email newsletter management

💡 Affiliate tip: Copy.ai has one of the most generous affiliate programmes around, offering 45% commission — which adds up quickly if you're already recommending it to business owner clients.

3. AI-Generated Digital Products

This one has serious passive income potential. AI can help you create digital products — eBooks, guides, templates, planners, and printables — that you sell once and earn from repeatedly.

Ideas that sell well on platforms like Etsy, Gumroad, or your own website: - Budget planners and financial trackers (huge demand in the UK right now) - Business plan templates for start-ups - CV and cover letter templates - Niche travel guides to UK destinations

Use AI to generate the initial content, then add your own knowledge and formatting to make it genuinely useful. Canva (which now has AI features) can handle the design side.

Realistic earning potential: Highly variable — from £50 to several thousand per month depending on your niche and marketing — but with minimal ongoing time investment once products are live.

4. YouTube Scriptwriting and Faceless Channels

Faceless YouTube channels — where you never appear on camera — are booming. Topics like personal finance, history, self-improvement, and UK property attract millions of views, and AI makes producing scripts at scale genuinely achievable.

Here's the workflow many creators use: 1. Research trending topics using tools like VidIQ or TubeBuddy 2. Generate a detailed script using Jasper AI or Copy.ai 3. Record a voiceover (or use an AI voice tool) 4. Edit using CapCut or DaVinci Resolve

Monetisation kicks in at 1,000 subscribers and 4,000 watch hours, but you can also earn from affiliate links in descriptions and sponsorships before that.

Realistic earning potential: £0–£100 in early months, scaling to £500–£3,000+ as your channel grows.

5. AI Chatbot Setup for Local Businesses

Many UK small businesses — restaurants, tradespeople, salons, estate agents — are completely unaware that they could have a basic AI chatbot on their website handling customer enquiries 24/7. And most have no idea how to set one up.

You can learn to build simple chatbots using tools like Tidio, ManyChat, or even custom GPT configurations, then charge local businesses a setup fee plus a monthly maintenance retainer.

Realistic earning potential: £200–£500 setup fee + £50–£150/month per client.

A Quick Note on Tax

If you're earning from a side hustle in the UK, HMRC wants to know about it. The good news is you have a £1,000 trading allowance each tax year before you need to report anything. Beyond that, you'll need to register as self-employed and complete a Self Assessment tax return.

Keep records of your income and any tool subscriptions (like Jasper or Copy.ai) — software costs are generally allowable business expenses.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any technical skills to start an AI side hustle?

Not really — most modern AI tools are designed for non-technical users. The biggest skill you need is the ability to write a clear prompt (instructions to the AI) and then edit the output so it sounds natural and accurate. Both improve quickly with practice.

How much can I realistically earn from an AI side hustle in the UK?

This varies enormously, but most people starting out can expect £200–£500 per month within their first few months if they're consistent. Scale that up with more clients or products, and £1,000–£3,000 per month is achievable within a year for the most dedicated side hustlers.

Which AI tool should I start with?

For writing and content-based side hustles, we'd recommend starting with Copy.ai (which has a free plan) to explore the basics, then upgrading to Jasper AI if you're taking on larger content workloads or client projects. Both are excellent — it depends on your use case.

Is it legal to sell AI-generated content?

Yes — in the UK, there's currently no law against selling AI-assisted content, and many professional services already use AI tools. The key is transparency with clients where relevant, and always ensuring the final output is accurate, original, and useful. Don't simply copy-paste raw AI output without reviewing it.

Will AI side hustles still be relevant in a few years?

Almost certainly yes — arguably more so. As AI continues to develop, the people who understand how to use it effectively will become more valuable, not less. Getting in now means building skills and a client base before the market matures.

Conclusion: Start Small, Think Big

The beauty of AI side hustles is that you can genuinely start this weekend. Pick one idea from this list, sign up for a free trial of an AI tool, and spend a few hours getting familiar with it. You don't need a business plan, a limited company, or a big audience — just a willingness to learn and a bit of time.

The people earning serious money from AI side hustles today are mostly ordinary people who simply started before everyone else caught on.

Ready to get going? Start with a free trial of Copy.ai for content creation, or explore Jasper AI if you're serious about scaling a writing or copywriting service. Both are genuinely excellent tools — and if you end up recommending them to clients, you can earn recurring commissions on top of your service income.
